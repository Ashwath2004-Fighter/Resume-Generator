Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear? +

Wear comfortable athletic clothing that allows full range of motion. Shorts or track pants with a breathable t-shirt work great. Bring good grip shoes for outdoor surfaces.

Is this for beginners? +

Absolutely! We have dedicated beginner workshops and activities for all fitness levels. Whether you've never done a push-up or you're an advanced athlete, there's something for you.

Do I need to register for all events? +

No, you can choose specific events during registration. You can participate in competitions, workshops, or both. Spectators are also welcome!

Will there be food and water? +

Yes! Water stations will be available throughout the venue. We'll also have healthy snacks and refreshments. Food stalls will be on-site for purchase.

Can I bring family/friends as spectators? +

Yes! Spectators are welcome and entry is free. Bring your friends and family to cheer you on and experience the energy of Davanagere's calisthenics community!
